How it works

How a Lofton
project happens.

Every project follows the same three phases. You work with the same designer at each one — no handoffs, no surprises.

01

Plan

Walk the property. Set the budget.

We start on your property, not in a showroom. A designer walks the site with you, listens to what you want, then comes back with a clear plan and a clear budget.

  • On-site consultation with a designer
  • Detailed plan and clear budget delivered
  • Follow-up meeting before any work begins
02

Design

Refine it until you love it.

Nothing gets built until you sign off on every detail. We iterate with you on plant choices, materials, and layout until the design is exactly what you want.

  • Concept design built around your goals
  • Internal design review for the details
  • Final design delivered for your approval
03

Install

Our team, start to finish.

Site prep, planting, hardscape, cleanup — all by our team. You meet your project lead before we break ground, and walk the finished space with them at the end.

  • Pre-install walkthrough with your project lead
  • Installation handled by our team
  • Post-install walkthrough to confirm we nailed it
